Objective

The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without exceeding 21.

Card Values

Face cards are worth 10, Aces can be worth 1 or 11, and other cards are worth their face value.

Gameplay

  • Each player is dealt two cards.
  • The dealer also gets two cards, with one face up.
  • Players can choose to "Hit" to get another card or "Stand" to keep their hand.
  • If a player's total exceeds 21, they "Bust" and lose the round.
  • The dealer must hit until they reach at least 17.
  • The winner is the one with the closest total to 21 without exceeding it.
